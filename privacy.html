Privacy Policy

iChanges – Jazz Chord Trainer does not collect, store or process personal data.

The app does not require a user account and does not use analytics, advertising or tracking services.

Audio samples may be downloaded from public content delivery networks during the first launch of the application. No personal information is transmitted or stored by the app.

All settings and progress statistics are stored locally on the user's device.
